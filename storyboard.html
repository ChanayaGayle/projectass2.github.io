Pages and their purpose:

  • Home Page

    The purpose of my home page for this individual assignment is to create an inviting and engaging entry point for every visitor. Through a chosen image and an introductory video of myself, I tried to introduce myself in a compelling way, offering insights into my passions and interests. Sharing select personal facts aims to establish a sense of authenticity and relatability. Ultimately, this page acts as a gateway, beckoning visitors to delve into the different aspects of my life and interests, encouraging exploration and fostering connections within the digital realm.

  • Profession Page

    The goal of my Profession Page is to create a comprehensive platform that showcases my professional journey and aspirations within the field of computer science. This page functions as a prospective resume, highlighting my academic pursuits as I work towards a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. It outlines the fundamental requirements and skills needed for success within this career industry. Additionally, it has my career vision, hoping to illustrating my passion for technology to drive innovation and positive change.

  • Personailty Page

    The purpose of my Personality Page is to provide insight into how my dominant traits, characterized by being a dedicated hard worker and procrastinator influence my academic life. Additionally, I pay tribute to my hero, my mother, for her enduring encouragement in my academic journey. By sharing the results of an online personality test categorizing me as a 'Guardian,' I demonstrate the complexity of personalities and question the comprehensive accuracy of such tests based on a limited information about an individual. It showcase a more profound understanding of me as a individual to my audince.

  • Person Developement Page

    The purpose for my Personal development page is to provide an in-depth look into my personal development, academic journey, and skill acquisition. It showcases my dedication and commitment to growth, both personally and in the field of Computer Science. The page features sections on key book takeaways from the twelve pillars that i hope will resonate with my life, reflection on implementing them for self-improvement, and details about my ongoing pursuit of a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. It aims to convey my aspirations and proficiency while offering visitors a comprehensive understanding of my journey and goals.



THEME

The theme of my website gives off a delightful and charming ambiance with a palette of pastel colors, including soft pinks, calming blues, light yellows, and gentle purples. The use of pastel shades imparts a soothing and tender aesthetic, creating a playful and cute feeling that aligns with a girlish charm.

The overall design and colors combine to create a visually appealing and inviting atmosphere, making visitors feel welcomed and immersed in a pleasant online experience. The target audience for this website primarily comprises young adults, preferably females, who are drawn to a cute, girlish aesthetic and have an affinity for pastel colors. The design and content are tailored to resonate with their tastes and preferences, aiming to create a space that they find visually appealing and relatable.

However, the website is inclusive and welcoming to anyone, regardless of age or gender, who appreciates or is open to this aesthetic. It embraces a diverse audience, ensuring that all visitors can enjoy and engage with the content, even if it is outside their usual preferences, promoting an inclusive and accepting online community.
