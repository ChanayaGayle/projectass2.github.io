My Personality



My strongest trait is my unwavering determination and hardworking nature. I constantly strive to achieve my academic goals, putting in the effort required to succeed. However, I'm aware of my tendency to procrastinate, which can sometimes hinder my progress. To manage this, I've been focusing on enhancing my time management skills, setting clear goals, and breaking down tasks into manageable parts. While I may not display my emotions prominently unless pushed to the extreme, I understand the importance of expressing them constructively, especially during stressful academic periods. My hobbies, such as reading, drawing, and listening to audiobooks, play a significant role in my academic life. Reading enriches my knowledge and critical thinking abilities, while drawing serves as a creative outlet, helping me destress and regain focus. Audiobooks, on the other hand, allow me to continue learning even while engaged in other activities, optimizing my time and enabling continuous self-improvement. Balancing these traits and hobbies is a continuous effort, but it has a positive impact on my academic journey, enhancing productivity, learning outcomes, and overall well-being throughout my educational experience.



My Hero:
My Mother

"You will be wounded many times in your life.
You’ll make mistakes. Some people will call them failures, but I have learned that failure is really God’s way of saying, Excuse me, you’re moving in the wrong direction.
It’s just an experience, just an experience." Oprah Winfrey




Although this uplifting quote didn't originate from my mother, she would often recite a portion of it whenever I faced challenging moments, aiming to lift my spirits and remind me that life isn't always a smooth journey. It's moments like these that have solidified my choice of her as my hero—her wisdom and support have been my guiding light during difficult times.







  Online Personality Test Result  







Reflecting on the given test, I believe that a test with a limited number of questions may not accurately and comprehensively assess someone's personality. Personality is a complex and multi-dimensional aspect of an individual, influenced by various factors that cannot be adequately captured in a brief test.

In my case, I feel the test made only surface-level assumptions and did not truly grasp the intricacies of my personality.
It may need a more comprehensive approach, considering diverse aspects of one's character and behavior.
Therefore, I think there's room for improvement in the design and structure of such tests to provide a more insightful and accurate understanding of an individual's personality.
